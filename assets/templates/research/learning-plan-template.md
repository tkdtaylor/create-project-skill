## Learning practices

- **Timebox research** — when stuck for more than 30 minutes on a concept, write down what you don't understand and move on. Return to it after the next reading.
- **Build before you feel ready** — the project phases are designed to be slightly beyond your comfort zone. That's intentional.
- **Write to learn** — the writing prompts aren't busywork. Explaining forces you to find gaps in your understanding.
- **Track progress** — check off prerequisites and topics as you go. If a topic takes much longer than expected, note why.
